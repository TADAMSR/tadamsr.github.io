Tea

Written by Thomas R. Adams

Verse 1

Can you be my cup of tea,
And me your glass of wine
Wet windshields on rainy days
Dull these tired eyes.
But your hand upon my back
Always wakes me up just fine 
Time to see if we can swim
As we sink into our lives.
