A Short Melody

Written by Thomas R. Adams

Verse 1

Your hopefulness in every lie,
Crossing your T’s and dotting your I’s.
Your open emotion you try to hide
Only works if you’re blind.

Verse 2

Your secrets sit behind a cheap disguise,
A smoke screen of memories that died.
Time to bury whatever’s left
And move along from your regret.

Pre-Chorus

You only got so much of the rope
You’re hangin’ on.
So hold on tight and don’t let your grip
All come undone.

Chorus

You don’t got so long
To carry on —
It ain’t worth wasting time.
Better pack your shit
And move out quick
With each minute passin’ you by.


Pre-Chorus

You only got so much of the rope
You’re hangin’ on.
So hold on tight and don’t let your grip
All come undone.

Outro (Slow)

You don’t got so long
To carry on —
And it ain’t worth wasting your time.
