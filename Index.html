Thomas R. Adams writes from the intersections of small-town storytelling, soul, and modern country. His catalog ranges from intimate heartbreak (“She’d Believe”) to spiritual nostalgia (“Sunday Psalms”) and groove-driven slow jams (“Sun Rays”).

This page highlights ten representative songs across styles — all written to be cut, covered, or developed in collaboration with artists, publishers, and producers.

Song Portfolio · Top 10
All songs written by Thomas R. Adams
  • She’d Believe
    Country · Heartbreak Ballad
    A regret-soaked confession from a man who knows he waited too long to change. Classic emotional country with vivid details and a strong, memorable hook built around “I’d be a fool… to think she’d believe.”
    Demo: Available (rough acoustic)
  • Sun Rays
    R&B · Soul · Slow Jam
    Neo-soul / retro-R&B slow jam that compares a former love to shifting light through seasons. Smooth, romantic, and groove-ready with visual lyrics — ideal for a soulful male or female vocal.
    Demo: Pending studio-style acoustic
  • Tea
    Indie Soul · Love Song
    A cozy, playful love song built on domestic imagery: rainy-day windshields, dancing in the living room, and candy-sweet metaphors. Intimate and hooky, suitable for pop, indie, or sync-friendly productions.
    Demo: To be recorded
  • GodSpeed
    Bluegrass / Country · Uplift
    A runaway redemption song — outrunning demons with “wings in a suitcase” and fueled by a prayer. Written for an energetic bluegrass / country band with strong chorus lift.
    Demo: To be recorded
  • A Short Melody
    Folk · Reflective
    Introspective folk piece in the spirit of Townes Van Zandt — a reminder that time is short and you can’t hang onto the rope forever. Minimal, poetic, and very lyric-driven.
    Demo: To be recorded
  • I Need a Keith Whitley Song
    Traditional Country · Tribute
    A modern barroom prayer wrapped in classic references: Whitley, Willie, Faron Young, Jerry Jeff Walker. Tailored for hardcore country listeners and artists who live in that tradition.
    Demo: To be recorded
  • Your Man
    Country · Modern Love
    A working-class love song about burnout, self-doubt, and finally becoming the man she deserves. Mid-tempo modern country with a strong chorus payoff: “Until I became your man.”
    Demo: To be recorded
  • Stale Cigarettes
    Country · Gritty Story Song
    Cinematic chase through rain, smoke, and bad habits. “Rain-soaked butts in my pocket” and “burning up my lungs before I hit that 24” set a vivid scene for an outlaw- leaning production.
    Demo: To be recorded
  • Sunday Psalms
    Country · Spiritual Nostalgia
    A warm, faith-rooted song about cigarettes, coffee, small-town church, and trying to get life back on track. Perfect for a heartfelt country artist looking for a spiritual but grounded cut.
    Demo: Acoustic demo available
  • Memory Worth Remembering
    Country · Emotional Mid-tempo
    Built around the central idea of wanting to be “a memory worth remembering” — postcards, VHS tapes, hand-me-down tools. A strong concept song with multiple visual hooks for modern or traditional country production.
    Demo: To be recorded
About & Contact

Thomas R. Adams is a Pennsylvania-based songwriter balancing full-time work, grad-school, and late-night writing sessions. His catalog spans classic country, Americana, folk, and soul-influenced slow jams — all built on narrative lyrics, emotional honesty, and strong melodic hooks.

He is actively seeking relationships with publishers, artists, producers, and collaborators looking for fresh material with a roots-driven, story-first approach.
