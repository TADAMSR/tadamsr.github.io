Sun Rays

Written by Thomas R. Adams

Chorus:
I see you in the sun rays,
My better half of my better days.
The smell of your perfume in the springtime,
Your fuzzy image penetrates my mind.

Verse 1:
Drink in the moonlight like a Zinfandel,
A candy-coated dream I inhale.
Your sweet concoction of love—
The rush I get when our lips touch.

Pre-Chorus:
Oh, the silent days and silent nights,
Memories of us skatin’ through life.
I wrestle these thoughts,
And oh, I realize that without you I’m lost.

Chorus:
But I still see you in the sun rays,
My better half of my better days.
Smile shine glarin’ off the ice in the wintertime,
Your words a poem that inscript my mind—
Sayin’:
Oh, I love you my darlin’,
Oh my darlin’, my love.
Whisper sweet love to me, darlin’—oh yeah.
You hold my heart in your arms—yeah.

Outro Verse 1:
And when the night fades into golden haze,
I feel your touch still callin’ my name.
Your silhouette soft in the evening light—
Goosebumps waltz down my spine.

Outro Verse 2:
And all through these changin’ seasons,
I’m holdin’ tight to every lovin’ reason.
Oh darlin’, for even when the daylight’s gone,
I close my eyes and still see you in the sun rays.
